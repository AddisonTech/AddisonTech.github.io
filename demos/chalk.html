Defense Recommended Adjustments

01 Cover-2 Robber

Rolls the safety into the intermediate zone to eliminate the TE seam. Forces the QB to hold the ball and take a check-down. Most effective against their 2-minute tempo.

2-Minute Coverage
02 Slant 3-Tech Pre-Snap

Move the 3-tech to an A-gap shade alignment pre-snap. Their center struggles with lateral shade movement, creating disruption before the snap count.
